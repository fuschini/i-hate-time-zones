I Hate Time Zones

A manifesto against the tyranny of local time.

There are not 24 time zones. There are 38. Because someone decided half-hour and quarter-hour offsets were a good idea. They were not.

Twice a year, most of the world agrees to confuse itself with daylight saving time. Arizona opted out, except for the Navajo Nation, which opted in, except for the Hopi Reservation inside the Navajo Nation, which opted back out.

UTC everywhere. Forever. That's all we ask.
